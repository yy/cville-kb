Two [[Topic/Smart Scale]] applications for ramp extensions at the **US 29 / US 250 interchange** near Ivy Road, under consideration in the FY 2026 round:

- **US 29 NB / US 250 EB on-ramp extension** — $13,865,000
- **US 29 SB / US 250 WB off-ramp extension** — $10,664,361

Combined: roughly **$24.5 million** for ramp work at a single interchange.

## MPO Technical Committee, 2026-04-07

Committee chair [[Person/Ben Chambers]] (Charlottesville's transportation planning manager) voted against advancing the projects, arguing *"the projects do not address our needs as the city or as a region in this corridor"* — framing them as a county/VDOT matter where the city should not spend its limited Smart Scale capacity. He pointed to the 5th Street Parkway / 5th Street intersection, site of a recent fatality, as a higher regional priority.

Votes:

- Motion to table both projects: **failed** 4–5 with 3 abstentions
- Recommend the northbound on-ramp: **passed** 5–3 with 3 abstentions
- Southbound off-ramp recommendation: **deferred** to the June meeting

Next steps: MPO Policy Board vote on **2026-04-22**; if approved, City Council vote on **2026-07-20**.
