The Water Street Parking Garage is a city-owned downtown parking structure. Posted rates are roughly $6 for 4 hours, conspicuously cheap relative to comparable downtown garages and to the city's reported outstanding debt on the structure (~$70M).

> [!question] Who is subsidizing the rates?
> [[Person/Brad Campbell|Brad Campbell]] flagged on Bluesky (2026-04-25) that the posted rates appear far below cost recovery given the outstanding debt. The implied gap suggests a substantial public subsidy for downtown parking.

## Open questions

- Outstanding debt on the garage — bond issuance history, refinancing, current principal
- Operating revenue vs. debt service vs. O&M — is the garage cash-flow negative?
- Who covers the gap (general fund, parking fund, TIF, other)?
- How posted rates compare to actual cost recovery and to nearby private garages
