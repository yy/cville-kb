Community-owned cooperative grocery store planned for the [[Project/Cville Food Co-op]] development in the Fifeville neighborhood — the former Estes IGA site.

- Website: https://thecvillefoodcoop.com/

## Overview

Consumer cooperative with democratically elected board. Members purchase a one-time membership share and gain voting rights, discounts, and profit-sharing. Accepts SNAP and WIC benefits, with pay-over-time membership plans.

## Formation

- 2024: Fifeville Neighborhood Association ran a six-month Cherry Avenue Grocery Community Engagement initiative
- January 2025: Multi-racial, multi-generational steering committee formed

## Partners

- Fifeville Neighborhood Association
- [[Organization/Piedmont Housing Alliance]]
- National Co-op Grocers (national supply chain)
- Local and regional farmers and producers
